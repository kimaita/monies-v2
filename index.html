See exactly where your money goes.

Monies automatically reads standard mobile money transaction messages and generates beautiful, easy-to-understand visualizations.

  • Custom Categories

    Assign labels like 'Groceries', 'Transport', or 'Rent'. Monies groups them beautifully.

  • Summary Statistics

    View your spending and inflow grouped by week, month, or specific categories.

Find any transaction in seconds.

Stop endlessly scrolling through your messages. Search, sort, and filter your entire financial history with powerful on-device queries.

Filter by Type

Instantly isolate merchant payments, peer-to-peer transfers, and withdrawals.

Subject & Direction

Search by sender or recipient name. Toggle between Money In and Money Out instantly.

CSV Export

Need it in Excel? Export your filtered searches directly to a CSV file to share or archive.

Engineered for clarity.

A clean, distraction-free architecture designed specifically for seamless mobile money tracking. No clutter, just insights.

Instant Parsing

Retrieves your messages and parses transaction amounts, dates, balances, and subjects in milliseconds entirely offline.

Smart Grouping

Transactions are organized effortlessly. Assign a category once, and easily see visual breakdowns of where you spend the most.

Data Portability

Your data isn't locked in our ecosystem. Export your entire transaction history, or just a filtered view, straight to CSV.

Your finances are
your business.

Reading SMS data is highly sensitive. That is why Monies is built on a strict zero-data policy. We don't want your data, and we can't see it.

No Internet Needed

Every calculation, search, and parse happens on your device's processor. No connection required.

No Cloud Servers

We do not sync your transactions to the cloud. What happens on your phone, stays on your phone.

No Accounts

Skip the sign-up. Just open the app, grant permissions, and start viewing your insights instantly.
